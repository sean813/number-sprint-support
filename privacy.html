Number Sprint Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Number Sprint is a mobile game created by Sean Norbury.

This privacy policy explains what information may be collected when you use Number Sprint.

Information We Collect

Number Sprint does not require you to create an account and does not ask you to enter personal information directly into the app.

The app uses Apple Game Center for multiplayer matchmaking and leaderboards. Game Center is provided by Apple and is subject to Apple’s own privacy practices.

The app also uses Google AdMob to show ads to users who have not purchased Remove Ads. AdMob may collect information such as device identifiers, advertising data, approximate location, app interaction data, diagnostics, and other information used to provide and measure ads.

Number Sprint includes an optional non-consumable in-app purchase called Remove Ads. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Number Sprint does not receive or store your payment card information.

How Information Is Used

Information may be used to:

  • provide Game Center multiplayer and leaderboard features
  • show and measure ads through Google AdMob
  • process and restore the Remove Ads purchase
  • improve app reliability and diagnose issues

Remove Ads

If you purchase Remove Ads, Number Sprint will stop showing ads in the app. The Google Mobile Ads SDK may still be included in the app, but ads are not loaded or shown for users with the Remove Ads purchase active.

Children’s Privacy

Number Sprint is intended as a general audience game. The app does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

Third-Party Services

Number Sprint uses third-party services, including:

  • Apple Game Center
  • Apple App Store In-App Purchases
  • Google AdMob

These services may process information according to their own privacy policies.
